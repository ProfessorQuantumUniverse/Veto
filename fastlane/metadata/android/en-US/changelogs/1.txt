First public release of Veto.

* A native, open source VirusTotal client
* Scan installed apps, files, and links
* Quick and Detailed report modes
* Plain language help on every section, toggleable in settings
* Full fidelity reports, including an Additional data section for everything the service returns
* Background scanning with progress and a completion notification
* Saved scans for offline viewing
* Rate limit notice
* Material 3 design with light and dark themes
